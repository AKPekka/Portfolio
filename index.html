About Me

Welcome to my portfolio. I am a Mechanical Engineer with a passion for creative problem solving and delivering real-world impact through design and manufacturing. I recently graduated from Purdue University, where I built a strong foundation in mechanical engineering and gained hands-on experience through internships at Tesla and Rivian. My background includes everything from advanced CAD and simulation to process optimization and project leadership, with a focus on driving manufacturable solutions in fast-paced environments.

I enjoy working at the intersection of innovation and practicality—translating ideas into robust, efficient systems alongside cross-functional teams. As a former board member and Director of Marketing for Purdue ASME, I also value mentorship and community. My goal is to keep learning, building, and contributing to projects that shape the future of engineering.

This collection showcases selected projects from my academic journey at Purdue University, highlighting my skills in design, analysis, prototyping, and problem-solving.

Professional Experience

Mechanical Design Engineering Intern

Tesla

May 2024 – Aug 2024

    At Tesla, I worked on the design and manufacturability of exterior trims and underbody components, using CATIA v5 and advanced simulation tools to validate and optimize sheet metal parts. My role centered on creating die surfaces and running DFM studies that improved both product quality and production speed. By leveraging shape morphing and FEA, I helped streamline new component launches and reduce design lead times.

Manufacturing Engineering Intern

My work directly supported successful production launches and the validation of new manufacturing systems.

Mechanical Engineering Intern
